What are exoplanets?

Exoplanets are planets that live far, far away from us, outside of our solar system! Just like how Earth and the other planets orbit our Sun, exoplanets orbit around different stars in space. Some of these planets can be very big, like giant gas balls, and some can be small, rocky, and a little like Earth.

What’s really cool is that scientists use special telescopes to look for these exoplanets, even though they are too far to see with our eyes. Some exoplanets are super hot, while others are freezing cold, and they can have colorful skies or strange moons!

People are excited about exoplanets because maybe, just maybe, there are planets out there where aliens or other forms of life might live. Imagine what that would be like!

Isn’t that amazing? There are so many exoplanets waiting to be discovered, and each one has its own story to tell!
